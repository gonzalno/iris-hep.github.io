## My research:
My current research focuses on the search for strongly interacting dark sectors using jet substructure and the development of quality assurance software for the ATLAS HL-LHC Global Common Module.

## My expertise is:
I am proficient in Python with experience in C++, Julia, and Javascript. I have also developed expertise in hardware communications and testing. I work often with jet clustering algorithms and understanding detector effects on the jet's substructure.
